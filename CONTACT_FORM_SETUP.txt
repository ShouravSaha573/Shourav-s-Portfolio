Contact Form Setup
==================

The website is already coded to use Web3Forms so visitors can send messages to your inbox without showing your receiving email address on the webpage.

To activate it:
1. Go to Web3Forms and create a free access key using your receiving email address.
2. Open index.html.
3. Find this line:
   <input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">
4. Replace YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY_HERE with your actual Web3Forms access key.
5. Save the file and upload/deploy the website again.

The form has only:
- From: sender's email
- Message: sender's message

Your receiving email address is not written in the webpage source. The public access key works like an alias/form ID for your email.
